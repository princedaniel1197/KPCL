# Sentinel — 7-Minute MD Walkthrough

One talking point per screen. The thesis, repeated on every stop: *your losses
hide in gaps between numbers that should match but are never reconciled —
Sentinel is the ledger that reconciles them.*

---

**0:00 — MD Dashboard (`/`)** — *"₹84 crore is at risk right now, and every rupee traces to a ledger line."*
Point at the hero numeral, then the three-way split: coal reconciliation
leakage, LD accrued but never claimed, and what KERC will likely disallow in
the draft filing. Scroll the alert ledger: fourteen live flags from seven
different departments on one screen — no officer today sees these together.
Flip the EN/ಕನ್ನಡ toggle once; switch the plant scope to RTPS and watch every
number re-cut.

**1:00 — Entity Graph (`/graph`)** — *"The same contractor failing in five silos is invisible until the silos share one graph."*
Click the gold **Deccan EPC Ltd** node. Dossier: three projects, two slipping;
₹4.2 cr LD un-pursued; one live arbitration; vendor grade D. Ask: who in the
corporation knew all five facts at once? Nobody — they live in five registers.

**2:00 — Coal debit note (`/coal/sources` → `/coal/ledger` → a W-3 rake)** — *"Colliery W-3 slips a full grade on 40% of its rakes — and the third-party samples prove the gap opens in transit."*
Sources league: W-3 tops the leakage league. Open a slipped rake: the GCV
waterfall shows billed 5,100 → received 4,460 against a 120 kcal norm. Press
**Generate debit note** — a print-ready, PSU-tone recovery instrument, drafted
by the ledger itself. Mention the comparators: this is the ₹1,400 cr/yr class
of leak at NTPC's auditors.

**3:00 — Project Control Tower (`/projects` → `/projects/clearances`)** — *"The schedule says 2030. The law says the forest gate is shut. Sentinel refuses to pretend otherwise."*
Control tower: RTPS U-1 R&M paid to 62% against 48% physical — the exact YTPS
failure mode the CAG documented. Then clearances: the Sharavathi-class PSP
pipeline shows FC-I blocked in red with a ₹206 cr mobilisation advance already
out against a frozen site, while monthly reports still claim road-formation
progress under a High Court stay.

**4:30 — Audit paras (`/regulatory/audit-paras`)** — *"31 paras are past the COPU clock — and the ATN drafts itself from the same ledger the auditor will ask about."*
Show the clock chips, open one overdue para, click *draft ATN* — the template
pulls the para's value, station, and owner. Then one tab over (`/regulatory`):
the prudence simulator lists exactly what KERC will disallow — ₹41 cr — with a
fix hint against each line, *before* filing instead of after.

**6:00 — Assistant (gold bubble, any page)** — *"Ask it what you'd ask your officers — it answers from the ledger, and only from the ledger."*
Ask **"Which contractor is slipping across projects?"** — it names Deccan with
the same five facts as the graph. Ask **"Draft the ATN for para 14/2025"**. Close
on the folio line at the foot of the page: every screen is synthetic data,
every counterparty fictional — the product is the reconciliation, not the
accusation.

**6:45 — Close** — *"Three spines — a reconciliation ledger, an obligation register, an entity graph. Pick any of the 45 modules as the entry product; the other 44 are configuration, not new builds."*
